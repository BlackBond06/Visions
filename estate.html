Estate Planning

We are dedicated, innovative, and professional when it comes to assisting you or your family with estate planning.

Recieve consultation

Our experienced and trusted tax and estate planning attorneys will represent your interests in all areas of tax and estate planning, including but is not limited to, the following:

Entity Related Planning
Family partnerships, joint ventures, and limited liability companies to develop and operate real estate or other business projects, substantially reducing the family’s potential income and estate tax while preserving control.

Trust Agreements
Living trusts, which provide for the disposition of a client’s assets at death in a tax-efficient and probate-free manner, and also for the management of property during the client’s lifetime in case of disability.

You or your family have access to the expertise of our professionals who work together to provide a complete solution and superior service.

Attorneys at Madiba Chambers practice in the areas of federal and state tax planning for persons and entities involved in real estate transactions, whether as owners, lenders, developers, foreign investors, or users.

We are also recognized for our expertise in estate planning and probate and trust administration.
